WORKING WITH FILES

In the box "Files" at the left side of the screen you can see the content of different folders by clicking on the 
minus sign if they are collapsed. For example, click on the minus sign of the OSF icon to view documents stored there.

To view a document, click on it.
To edit a document: 
1. View the document by clicking on it.
2. Click on the download button that appears at the top.
3. Edit the document on your computer.
4. Upload the revised document by drag­and­dropping it in the folder it came from. This will create a new version of the document. Note that the previous version will not be deleted. Version history is automatically added ­ you dont have to rename the document.
